Dumplings

Delicate, bite-sized treats with a savoury filling wrapped in a thin layer of dough.

Ingredients:

  • 120g All purpose flour
  • 120mL Water
  • 2tsp Salt(for dumpling wrappers)
  • 250g Ground pork
  • 20g Chopped cabbage
  • 3tsp Ground black pepper
  • 1tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2tbsp Sesame oil
  • 1tbsp Minced garlic
  • 1/2tbsp Minced ginger
  • 1 large egg
  • 30g chopped scallions
  • 1/2 tbsp salt(for the filling)

Procedure:

  • Step 1- Sieve the flour into a large bowl and whisk in the salt
  • Step 2- Pour the water into the flour and mix it with a spoon to form a shaggy dough
  • Step 3- Place the dough onto the counter and knead for 5 minutes until smooth and springy. Set aside
  • Step 4- Into a separate bowl, add the ground pork, cabbage, salt, black pepper, garlic, ginger, scallions, sesame oil, soy sauce and the egg, and mix thoroughly
  • Step 5- Pour 500mL of water into the steamer and set the heat to low
  • Step 6- To make the dumpling wrappers, tear off 30g pieces of dough and roll out into thin circular wrappers
  • Step 7- Scoop one spoon of filling anf place it in the center of the wrappers, and form dumplings
  • Step 8- Place the dumplings into the steamer and steam for 10 minutes

Enjoy~
